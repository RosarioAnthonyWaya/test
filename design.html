frequently asked questions

get clarity on our services and process

What services do you offer?

We specialize in designing unique, memorable logos for packaging across various industries. Our services include branding consultation, logo creation, packaging design concepts, and guidelines for brand application.

How long does the design process take?

The timeline depends on the scope of the project. On average, our projects take about 6-8 weeks. Complex packaging projects may take longer, and we’ll always provide a timeline after our initial consultation.

What is the typical cost for a packaging project?

Our pricing varies depending on the project's scope, including the level of detail and specific packaging requirements. Contact us for a custom quote tailored to your needs.

How many logo concepts will I receive?

As part of our unique process we are able to dive deep into your company needs. As such when it comes to design we typically provide 1-3 distinct concepts based on your unique brand vision and preferences. Along the journey you will provide feedback and refine your preferred concept.

What file formats will I receive for my logo?

We deliver logos in various formats to cover all your packaging needs, including .AI, .EPS, .PNG, and .PDF files. If you need a specific format, just let us know.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes, our packages include up to three rounds of revisions to ensure the design perfectly meets your vision. Additional revisions can be arranged if necessary.

Do you offer additional services such as Brand Strategy and Websites?

Yes, Packabo is a sister agency to the award winning Brandlucent branding agency (who specialize in Brand Strategy Brand Identity) and Awescape web agency (who specialize in digital brand experiences). That's to say, we got you covered!

How do I get started?

Contact us to set up an initial consultation. We’ll discuss your goals for your project and will soon be crafting the perfect logo and designs for your packaging needs.
